We are a friendly, helpful and social Free Company for Final Fantasy XIV located on the Behemoth server. To us, this game is about the people that you share your journey with. Beach parties? You read that right. No matter the week, there is always something going on within MOG. Overall, together we strive to spread positivity throughout the game and help each other grow.
